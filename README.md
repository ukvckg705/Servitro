## Servitro Reviews

Servitro has established itself in the budget VPS hosting community with a mixed but generally positive reputation. The company has been featured multiple times on LowEndBox, a respected platform in the budget hosting space, which speaks to their legitimacy in the market.

**Positive Feedback:**

Community members on LowEndBox and LowEndTalk forums appreciate Servitro's transparent pricing and straightforward approach. Users particularly value the high-performance AMD processors, genuine 10Gbps network connectivity, and the exceptional value proposition of their dollar-a-month offerings. The acceptance of both PayPal and cryptocurrency payment methods has been well-received by the international customer base.

The provider's transparency about their no-RAID configuration has been praised as honest and upfront, helping customers understand the importance of maintaining their own backups. Many users in the budget hosting community see this as a refreshing approach compared to providers who hide infrastructure details.

**Areas for Improvement:**

Servitro's Trustpilot profile shows a 3.0-star rating based on limited reviews, indicating mixed customer experiences. Some users have reported issues with customer support responsiveness, particularly around service activation times and technical issue resolution. There have been complaints about infrastructure changes being made without advance notice, including IP address changes.

As a relatively new provider that launched in 2025, Servitro is still building its track record. The company typically responds to negative reviews within one week, showing engagement with customer feedback. However, some users have expressed concerns about support ticket response times and the resolution of technical problems.

**Community Presence:**

Servitro maintains an active presence on LowEndTalk under the handle @Servitro, where they engage with the community and address questions. The company has run multiple promotional campaigns and giveaways, contributing to community engagement.

**Recommendation:**

Servitro appears best suited for users who understand budget hosting limitations and maintain proper backups. The pricing is genuinely competitive, and the hardware specifications are impressive for the cost. However, potential customers should be aware that this is a young company still establishing its reliability record. Consider starting with a short-term commitment to evaluate whether the service meets your specific needs.
